### Vorschlag: Vorgehen bei Writing
1. Aufgabenstellung analysieren:
	1. Um welches Format handelt es sich?
	2. In welche Teile muss ich die Writing gliedern
	3. Oft wird bei den Aufgabenstellungen die Zeitform indirekt angegeben (bsp.: Write about why you want*ed* to go to school -> Simple Past; Next weekend -> Future)
	4. In der Aufgabenbeschreibung wird angegeben in welcher Situation man sich befindet

### Vorschlag: Wie beginnt man mit einem Blog Eintrag
- Einleitung: 
	- In this blog article, I want to talk about (X) and why I wanted to visit it;
	- Scotland is a beautiful country, that is also the reason why I wanted to visit it.
	- Scotland is such a nice country, everyone must visit it!
- Hauptteil:
	- Alle Punkte in der Beschreibung abarbeiten (LINKING WORDS: but, because, and, also, so, First-ly, Second-ly, Third-ly, after all, already, just)
- Schluss:
	- I hope this blog post was helpful to you.
	- I hope you learned sth. about Scotland

### EMail:
- Hi there / Hello (name) / Dear Ladies and Gentlemen, (Unknown addressee) / Hello everyone
- Einleitung/Introduction: How are you. / Thought I would write to tell you / Have you heard the latest news
- Hauptteil: First of all , Secondly, Third, At last/ Connection words: but, because, so, because of that
- Schlussteil: When will you come visit me again? / I hope to hear from you again so. / I hope you are doing well / I'm looking forward to your reply
